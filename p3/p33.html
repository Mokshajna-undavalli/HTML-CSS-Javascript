Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel written by Chugong. It was first serialized in 2016 and later adapted into a webtoon (manhwa) in 2018, which became incredibly popular worldwide. The series is known for its thrilling action, overpowered protagonist, and stunning artwork.

Plot Summary
The story is set in a world where mysterious portals, called gates, connect Earth to dungeons filled with monsters. To fight these creatures, humans gain supernatural abilities and become Hunters.
The protagonist, Sung Jin-Woo, is an E-rank Hunter, the weakest of all, struggling to survive and support his family. However, after a near-death experience in a high-level dungeon, he receives a unique ability called the System, which allows him to level up infinitely—something no other Hunter can do. As he grows stronger, Jin-Woo uncovers the secrets of the gates, the origin of his powers, and the real threat looming over humanity.
