The Beginning After the End

Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel written by Chugong. It was first serialized in 2016 and later adapted into a webtoon (manhwa) in 2018, which became incredibly popular worldwide. The series is known for its thrilling action, overpowered protagonist, and stunning artwork.

Plot Summary
The narrative centers on King Grey, who, after a mysterious death, is reincarnated as Arthur Leywin in a world brimming with magic and mythical creatures. Retaining his previous life's wisdom, Arthur embarks on a journey to rectify past mistakes and uncover his true purpose in this new realm.
Arthur Leywin, the protagonist of The Beginning After the End (TBATE), is the reincarnation of King Grey, a powerful yet lonely ruler from a war-driven world. After dying under mysterious circumstances, he is reborn in Dicathen, a land filled with magic, where he is raised by loving parents and given a second chance at life. Retaining his past life's wisdom, Arthur quickly becomes an exceptionally skilled mage, possessing multiple elemental affinities, advanced combat skills, and a deep bond with his dragon companion, Sylvie. Though highly intelligent and strategic, he struggles with the burden of power, often feeling responsible for protecting those around him. Throughout his journey, he forms strong relationships, particularly with his childhood friend Tessia Eralith, and learns to balance his immense strength with emotional growth. Arthur’s story is one of redemption, self-discovery, and the pursuit of a fulfilling life beyond power alone.
