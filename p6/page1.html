"The Nightingale" by Kristin Hannah
Kristin Hannah's "The Nightingale" is a historical novel that delves into the lives of two sisters in Nazi-occupied France during World War II. This deluxe edition has been a consistent bestseller, reflecting its enduring popularity among readers.

"James" by Percival Everett
Percival Everett's novel "James" offers a profound exploration of identity and societal constructs. Its thought-provoking narrative has earned it a place among the top fiction bestsellers.

"Iron Flame" (The Empyrean #2) by Rebecca Yarros
The second book in the Empyrean series, "Iron Flame," continues to enchant readers with its rich world-building and compelling characters. Its popularity is evident from its strong sales figures.

"Bonded in Death" by J.D. Robb
J.D. Robb's "Bonded in Death" is a thrilling addition to the In Death series, blending mystery, suspense, and futuristic elements. Its gripping plot has made it a favorite among readers.
