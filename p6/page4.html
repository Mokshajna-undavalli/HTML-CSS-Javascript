Best Selling Books

"Sunrise on the Reaping" by Suzanne Collins
Suzanne Collins returns with "Sunrise on the Reaping," a gripping continuation of her renowned dystopian series. This novel delves deeper into the complexities of a society grappling with rebellion and the quest for freedom. Fans have eagerly embraced this installment, propelling it to the top of bestseller lists.

"Everything Is Tuberculosis: The History and Persistence of Our Deadliest Infection" by John Green
In this compelling non-fiction work, John Green explores the history and ongoing impact of tuberculosis. Combining meticulous research with personal narratives, Green sheds light on the resilience of this deadly infection and its implications for global health.

"The Fall Risk" by Abby Jimenez
Abby Jimenez's "The Fall Risk" is a contemporary romance that intertwines humor and heartfelt emotion. The story follows two individuals navigating unexpected challenges and finding love in unforeseen circumstances. Readers have praised its relatable characters and engaging plot.

"Wild Side" by Elsie Silver
"Wild Side" by Elsie Silver is a captivating romance set against the backdrop of the equestrian world. The narrative explores themes of passion, trust, and the pursuit of dreams, resonating with readers who appreciate heartfelt storytelling.

"Dream Count" by Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie
Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie's "Dream Count" is a profound exploration of identity, culture, and the complexities of modern life. Her eloquent prose and insightful commentary have solidified this novel's place among the top literary works of the year.
